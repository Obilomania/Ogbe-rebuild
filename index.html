OUR STORY

The name OGBE started within group of young individuals at a very tender age, residing in GOWON ESTATE of Egbeda, Akowonjo precisely. At first when it stated, the status-quo was quite different from what it later developed into but the watch word from the inception has always been "ONE LOVE". Membership of OGBE initially was drawn from members within the above mentioned estate as at then but presently the membership has revolved to both within and diaspora. OGBE is not just a group but a FAMILY derived from different biological parents but United by virtue of a strong bond that coexist within them, It's indeed a group of extra ordinary visionary men with the foresight of empowering it's members in this present dispensation we exist in. OGBE is a non religious, tribal and political group. Members within this group are free to participate in any political activities, religious activities and co. OGBE is a social interactive group where all members are equal irrespective of ones status but respect for elders and constituted authority is always adhered to. OGBE believes in ALL FOR ONE AND ONE FOR ALL.
